I amm the author of three books of poetry: Night Music (2018), Udon by the Remarkables (2016) and Moonshot (2008).

My poetry has appeared in numerous journals in New Zealand and overseas. I am also the co-author, with Latika Vasil, of the book Asperger Syndrome, Adolescence, and Identity: Looking Beyond the Label.

My most recent work is the short fiction Fury published by Idle Ink.
